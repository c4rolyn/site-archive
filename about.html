About the Artist

C. Paplham is a freelance illustrator and graphic designer. At present, she creates work for clients and invests time in developing her own illustrations, designs, and stories. In the future, she'd like to both publish her own comics and work on designing movies and television shows. She'd also like to be an explorer of time and space, but until that's technologically possible, she's stuck adventuring via pen and paper (and the occasional road trip).

Her clients include St. Norbert College and Khrome, and she is presently illustrating a book for a local author.
